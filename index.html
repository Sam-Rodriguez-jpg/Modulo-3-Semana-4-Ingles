BALLADS 1

Release Date: October 26, 2018

What is about?

Ballads 1, an album that deals with the concept of breaking a chain as its main metaphor. The person always feels tied to something and during each ballad the person enters a process of breaking the chain but never succeeds, not even at the end of the album.

SMITHEREENS

Release Date: November 4, 2022

What is about?

Smithereens, an album with a very interesting story, covers heartbreak in its maximum splendor and focuses a lot on relapses, how to have an idea of improvement but always end up falling back into the same patterns, it feels like an incomplete album, because in the end the protagonist after relapsing so much, is aware that he no longer wants that, but the album does not conclude with overcoming, it only remains in being aware but not beyond.
